Catching lung cancer early using only a sample of blood

We developed a method for the early detection of lung cancer by analysing free DNA circulating in the bloodstream.

Is fire smoke exposure linked to cancer-associated cfDNA changes?

We applied the analysis of  free DNA in blood  to investigate the risk of cancer in the firefighters that attended the Grenfell Tower fire.

Gut brain communication, inflammation and Parkinson’s disease

Blocking inflammation in the gut of Parkinson’s disease models protected the brain.

Folic acid: a new lead in treating Charcot-Marie-Tooth disease
